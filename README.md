BengalTravels

A West Bengal Travel Exploration App

BengalTravels is a simple and interactive travel web application built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It helps users explore the beauty, culture, and diversity of West Bengal through curated sections that cover both popular and lesser known aspects of the region.

Features

Clean and responsive user interface
Simple navigation across different travel categories
Well organized content for easy exploration
Lightweight and fast with no external frameworks

Sections Overview---------------------
---------Destination----------

This section showcases the most popular tourist locations in West Bengal including hill stations, beaches, and major cities. It is intended to help users plan their primary travel itinerary.

-----------Hidden Place------------

Focused on lesser known locations, this section highlights offbeat destinations that offer unique and less crowded travel experiences.

------------Cuisine----------

Covers the diverse food culture of West Bengal, including traditional meals and local specialties that define the region’s culinary identity.

----------Dessert---------

Dedicated to the famous sweets of Bengal such as rasgulla, sandesh, and mishti doi. This section emphasizes the importance of desserts in Bengali culture.

---------Spiritual--------

Includes important religious and spiritual places such as temples, mosques, and churches across the state.

---------Wild Life----------

Provides information about national parks, wildlife sanctuaries, and natural reserves for users interested in nature and biodiversity.

------------Culture-------------

Highlights festivals, art, music, and traditions that represent the cultural richness of West Bengal.

------------Heritage---------------

Focuses on historical landmarks, architectural sites, and places that reflect the historical legacy of the region.

------------Tech Stack----------

HTML5 for structure
CSS3 for styling
JavaScript for interactivity
